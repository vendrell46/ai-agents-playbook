# Trinity — Outcomes & Results

## Opportunity Detection

- **2 scans/day** across multiple signal sources
- 5-perspective advisory board scoring on every signal
- Weekly deep review with Opus cross-referencing patterns
- Maintains: Idea Ledger (opportunities log), World Model (belief/assumption tracking), Trinity Profile (personal context)

## Education

- **4 lessons/day** delivered consistently
- Finance track: adaptive progression through investing, tax, and portfolio topics
- eMBA track: 112 topics across 8 modules, adaptive difficulty
- All progress tracked locally for continuity across sessions

## Value

Trinity's main value isn't any single signal — it's the **consistency of exposure**. Scanning for opportunities 2x/day with structured evaluation means nothing slips by unnoticed. The education system turns dead time (morning coffee, evening wind-down) into structured learning.
